COVID-19 News Fact Checker

The Dataset

  • Subset of the CoAid Dataset
  • Diverse COVID-19 healthcare misinformation dataset
  • 1,127 real and 266 fake news samples

Preprocessing

  • Text & Content
  • Remove Stopwords, Lemmatize
  • TF-IDF
  • Undersampling

Model

  • Bayesian logistic regression
  • Incorporate uncertainty into our estimates
  • Performed using Pyro Library
